# Routes Map

## Primary Routes (Phase 1)
- `/` — homepage, reassurance + orientation
- `/about` — philosophy, values, therapeutic framework
- `/services` — service formats and practical details
- `/therapist` — therapist profile, qualifications, approach
- `/faq` — practical and emotional uncertainty reduction
- `/contact` — confidential enquiry and next-step flow

## Future Routes
- `/blog` — educational content and trust-building articles
- `/resources` — downloadable or evergreen support materials
- `/book` — dedicated booking funnel
- `/privacy-policy` — legal privacy details
- `/terms` — legal terms and conditions

## Metadata Pattern
- Route-level title/description for all pages
- Canonical URLs per page
- OG/Twitter cards for key conversion pages
