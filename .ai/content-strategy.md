# Content Strategy

## Brand Voice
- calm
- compassionate
- clinically professional
- culturally aware
- precise, never dramatic

The user should feel respected, not marketed to.

## Core Messaging Priorities
1. You can receive therapy in Turkish and/or English.
2. Your privacy and dignity are protected.
3. The service is professional, structured, and trustworthy.
4. Starting therapy can be gradual and low-pressure.

## Page-by-Page Guidance

### `/`
Purpose: immediate trust and orientation.
- Headline: reassurance + bilingual relevance.
- Subcopy: who this is for and why it is different.
- CTA: “Book an initial consultation” / “Send a confidential enquiry”.

### `/about`
Purpose: practice philosophy and values.
- Explain therapeutic stance, ethics, and care model.
- Emphasize cultural nuance without overstatement.

### `/services`
Purpose: clarity on offerings and fit.
- List service formats and what each includes.
- Explain expectations, not promises.

### `/therapist`
Purpose: professional credibility and relational safety.
- Credentials, training, modalities, language capabilities.
- Human warmth balanced with clear boundaries.

### `/faq`
Purpose: reduce uncertainty.
- Answer practical concerns (confidentiality, session process, language, fees).
- Keep answers concise and plain-language.

### `/contact`
Purpose: low-friction next step.
- Offer a gentle form and clear response-time expectations.
- Reassure confidentiality and non-judgmental support.

## Headline Style Rules
- Specific over abstract.
- Calm authority over poetic vagueness.
- Avoid superlatives and promotional claims.

## CTA Wording Rules
Preferred verbs: Book, Ask, Speak, Discuss, Enquire.
Avoid: Buy, Hurry, Unlock, Transform instantly.

## Premium Healthcare Copy Rules
- Never exploit fear.
- Never guarantee outcomes.
- Avoid stigma-reinforcing language.
- Use person-first and respectful phrasing.
