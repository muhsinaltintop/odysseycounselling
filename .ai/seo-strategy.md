# SEO Strategy

## Primary Keyword Targets
- Turkish therapist UK
- Turkish counselling London
- Turkish psychologist London
- Turkish speaking therapist UK
- bilingual counselling UK

## SEO Positioning Principles
- Prioritize relevance and trust over volume-chasing.
- Use natural language aligned with healthcare sensitivity.
- Optimize service + geography + language intent.

## Metadata Structure
Per route, define:
- title (clear intent + location/language relevance)
- meta description (calm, practical, human)
- canonical URL
- Open Graph title/description/image
- Twitter card basics

## Title Strategy
Pattern examples:
- `Turkish Counselling in London | Odyssey Counselling`
- `Bilingual Therapy Services (Turkish & English) | Odyssey Counselling`

Rules:
- keep titles specific and non-spammy
- include core service intent
- avoid repetitive keyword stuffing

## Schema Opportunities
- `Organization` (brand + contact + location context)
- `LocalBusiness` or relevant healthcare-practice schema (as legally appropriate)
- `FAQPage` schema for FAQ route
- `Article` schema for blog content

## Internal Linking Plan
- Homepage links to all core trust pages.
- Service pages link to therapist and contact.
- FAQ answers link contextually to services/contact.

## Future Blog Plan
Content clusters:
1. Starting therapy as a Turkish-speaking adult in the UK
2. Bilingual identity and emotional expression
3. Confidentiality and what first sessions are like
4. Anxiety, burnout, and migration-related stress

Each article should:
- answer one clear user question
- include gentle next-step CTA
- link to relevant service/contact pages
